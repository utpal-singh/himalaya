Welcome To MGM

Mahatma Gandhi Mission Trust was established on December 20, 1982, in Nanded, Maharashtra, with a futuristic vision to provide qualitative education and health services in the underdeveloped Marathawada region. Since inception, the trust has focused on providing healthcare services, school education, and higher education with dedication and commitment. In the course of time, MGM extended its services to Aurangabad, Navi Mumbai, and Parbhani in Maharashtra, and Noida in Uttar Pradesh. A chain of schools and colleges in the faculties of engineering, architecture, medical, nursing, management, computer science and IT, biotechnology, bioinformatics, fine arts, and journalism stand testimony to the endeavors of the trust. Mahatma Gandhi Mission has been instrumental in promoting classical dance and music for art seekers. The Trust has also paid heed to the aspect of physical fitness by promoting sports activities and creating the facilities for them. Services to society at the grass-roots level have been the basic vocation of the trust, along with education. The Trust has been instrumental in providing prompt and efficient healthcare services to the economically weaker section of a society. The Trust's hospitals and medical colleges underscore its commitment to human resource development, social health, and welfare.



About College

Mahatma Gandhi Mission’s College of Computer Science and IT is located in Nanded, a district in Maharashtra. The college was established in 2000. It is approved by the Government of Maharashtra and affiliated with Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University, Nanded.
Nanded city is well connected by road, rail, and air to all important cities in India. It has the unique historical importance of being the site of the Court and Congregation of the 10th Guru of the Sikhs, Shri Guru Gobind Singhji. Pilgrims from all over the world visit Nanded. The campus is located on a hilltop along the Nanded-Nagpur highway, approximately 3 kilometer's from the railway station and state bus station.
MGM Campus is spread over 20 acres and can be easily distinguished by its cleanliness, greenery, and beauty. The serene surroundings empower the atmosphere for learning.
