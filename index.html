Welcome To Himalaya Group of Institutions

At Himalaya College of Nursing, we are dedicated to providing exceptional nursing education and training. Situated in the picturesque surroundings of Chiksi, Sigori, Paliganj, Patna, Bihar, our institution is committed to preparing skilled and competent nursing professionals who can meet the global challenges of the healthcare industry. As a part of the esteemed Himalaya Educational Trust, our focus goes beyond traditional nursing education. We strive to blend the best practices in nursing with the advancements in technology, ensuring that our students are well-equipped to thrive in the ever-evolving healthcare landscape. Our experienced faculty members are passionate about nurturing the next generation of nursing professionals. They provide personalized guidance and mentorship to help our students achieve their full potential. With a comprehensive curriculum and state-of-the-art facilities, we foster an environment where theoretical knowledge meets practical training, preparing our students for success. At Himalaya College of Nursing, we believe in the transformative power of education. We are committed to empowering our students, especially girls, women, SCs, STs, and rural youth, by developing their entrepreneurial skills alongside their nursing expertise. We strive to create well-rounded individuals who can contribute to the healthcare industry and society as a whole. Whether you aspire to work in government institutions, private healthcare settings, or even venture into entrepreneurship, our programs are designed to equip you with the necessary skills and knowledge. Through our strong clinical partnerships, you will gain hands-on experience in real healthcare settings, enabling you to apply what you've learned in a practical context. We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our programs, faculty, facilities, and the holistic student life experience at Himalaya Group of Institutions. Feel free to reach out to our admissions office for any inquiries or to schedule a campus visit. Join us on this incredible journey towards a rewarding nursing career and a brighter future. Welcome to the Himalaya Group of Institutions, where excellence in nursing education meets limitless opportunities!



About College

The Himalaya College of Nursing, established by the Himalaya Educational Trust, is located in Chiksi, Sigori, Paliganj, Patna, Bihar, PIN-801110. Our institution is dedicated to meeting the global challenges of nursing education and training while integrating technology and higher learning strategies. We understand the importance of addressing the national norms in nursing education and training to support the medical profession effectively.
Himalaya Group of Institutions Campus is spread over 60 acres and can be easily distinguished by its cleanliness, greenery, and beauty. The serene surroundings empower the atmosphere for learning.
