Welcome To MGM

Mahatma Gandhi Mission Trust was established on 20th December 1982 in Nanded, Maharashtra with a futuristc vision to provide qualitative education and health services in the underdeveloped Marathawada region. Since inception, the trust has focused on providing healthcare services, school edution and higher education with dedication and commitment. In the course of time MGM extended its services to Aurangabad, Navi-Mumbai and Parbhani in Maharashtra and Noida (Utter Pradesh). A chain of schools and colleges in the faculties of Engineering, Architecture, Medical, Nursing, Management, Computer Science and IT, Biotechnology, Bioinformatics, Fine Arts and Journalism stand testimony to endeavors of the trust. Mahatma Gandhi Mission has been instrumental in promoting Classical Dance and Music for art seekers. The Trust has also paid heed to the aspect of physical fitness by promoting sports activities and creating the facilities for same. Services to the society at the grass root level have been the basic vocation of the trust along with education. The Trust has been instrumental in providing prompt and efficient healthcare services to the economically weaker section of a society the Trusts hospitals and Medical colleges underscore its commitment to human resource development, social health and welfare.

About College

Mahatma Gandhi Missionâ€™s College of Computer Science and IT is located at Nanded, a district place in Maharashtra. The College is established in the year 2000. It is approved by Government of Maharashtra and is affiliated to Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University, Nanded. Nanded city is well connected by road, rail and air to all important cities in India. It has unique historical importance of being the place of Court and Congregation of 10th Guru of Sikhs Shri Guru Gobind Singhji. Pilgrims all over the world visit Nanded. The campus is situated at around 3 Km from railway station and state transport Bus station, on a hilltop located at Nanded- Nagpur highway. MGM Campus is spread over 20 acres and can be easily distinguished with its cleanliness, greenery and beautifulness. The serene surrounding empowers the atmosphere for learning.
